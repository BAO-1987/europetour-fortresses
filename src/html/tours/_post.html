Royal Castles of France

In-Depth Description

Immerse yourself in the elegance and grandeur of France’s most famous royal castles. This tour offers a journey through history, art, and culture as you explore magnificent palaces and their stunning gardens. Each castle tells a unique story of French royalty and architectural brilliance.

Detailed Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival in Paris
  • Check-in at a luxury hotel.
  • Welcome dinner and briefing about the tour.
  • Day 2: Château de Chambord
  • Travel to the Loire Valley.
  • Guided tour of Château de Chambord, known for its distinctive French Renaissance architecture.
  • Free time to explore the gardens.
  • Day 3: Château de Chenonceau
  • Visit Château de Chenonceau, famous for spanning the River Cher.
  • Learn about its rich history and enjoy a leisurely walk through the beautiful gardens.
  • Day 4: Château de Versailles
  • Return to Paris.
  • Full-day tour of the opulent Château de Versailles, including the Hall of Mirrors and the extensive gardens.
  • Day 5: Departure from Paris
  • Free morning for last-minute shopping or sightseeing.
  • Departure.

Additional Information

  • Accommodations: 5-star hotels in Paris and the Loire Valley.
  • Transportation: Private luxury coach.
  • Meals: Daily breakfasts, welcome dinner, and select lunches.
  • Guides: Expert local guides with in-depth knowledge of French history and architecture.
  • Optional Activities: Wine tasting in the Loire Valley, a night tour of Paris.
