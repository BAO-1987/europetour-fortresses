Medieval Castles of Germany

In-Depth Description

Step into a fairy tale as you explore Germany’s medieval castles. This tour takes you through picturesque landscapes and historic fortresses, offering a glimpse into the medieval life of knights and kings. Discover the charm and mystery of Germany’s most iconic castles.

Detailed Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival in Munich
  • Check-in at a luxury hotel.
  • Welcome dinner and tour introduction.
  • Day 2: Neuschwanstein Castle
  • Travel to Bavaria.
  • Guided tour of Neuschwanstein Castle, the inspiration for Disney’s Sleeping Beauty castle.
  • Enjoy the stunning alpine scenery.
  • Day 3: Hohenzollern Castle
  • Visit Hohenzollern Castle, an impressive fortress atop Mount Hohenzollern.
  • Explore its museum filled with royal artifacts.
  • Day 4: Eltz Castle
  • Journey to the Moselle Valley.
  • Tour Eltz Castle, one of Germany’s best-preserved medieval castles.
  • Walk through its lush surroundings.
  • Day 5: Return to Munich and Departure
  • Free time in Munich for sightseeing or shopping.
  • Departure.

Additional Information

  • Accommodations: Luxury hotels in Munich and near the castles.
  • Transportation: Private coach.
  • Meals:Daily breakfasts, welcome dinner, and select lunches.
  • Guides: Knowledgeable guides specializing in medieval history.
  • Optional Activities: River cruise in the Moselle Valley, brewery tour in Bavaria.
