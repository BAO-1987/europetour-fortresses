• What types of tours do you offer?

    We offer a variety of tours, including guided tours of historic castles, romantic getaways to estates, family-friendly tours, and special themed tours such as haunted castle tours and holiday events.

  • How do I book a tour?

    You can book a tour directly on our website by visiting the "Book Now" page. Simply select your desired tour, choose your dates, and complete the booking form. You will receive a confirmation email with all the details.

  • What is included in the tour package?

    Our tour packages typically include transportation, guided tours, entrance fees to castles and estates, and some meals. Specific inclusions are listed on each tour's detailed page.

  • Can I customize my tour?

    Yes, we offer customizable tour options. If you have specific preferences or requirements, please contact us directly, and our team will work with you to create a personalized itinerary.

  • Are your tours suitable for children?

    Many of our tours are family-friendly and suitable for children. We also offer specific tours designed with younger visitors in mind. Please check the tour descriptions for more information or contact us with any questions.

  • What is your cancellation policy?

    Our cancellation policy varies depending on the tour. Generally, cancellations made 30 days before the tour date are fully refundable. Cancellations made within 30 days may incur a fee. Please refer to the specific tour's terms and conditions for detailed information.

  • Do you offer group discounts?

    Yes, we offer discounts for group bookings. If you are planning to book for a group, please contact us for more information on available discounts and special offers.

  • What should I bring on the tour?

    We recommend bringing comfortable walking shoes, weather-appropriate clothing, a camera, and any personal items you may need. Detailed packing suggestions are provided in your tour confirmation email.
